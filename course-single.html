    Course Details

    BBA Logistics Management is a 3-year undergraduate course in management that deals with the activities that are related to the planning, execution and control of the smooth and consistent flow of goods.

    Eligibility

    Students who have opted for commerce at the 10+2 level with Mathematics as a core subject are eligible to apply for admission to BBA Logistics Management Course. Candidates must also have obtained a minimum aggregate of 50%.
